About

Veronica Mumm brings over 25 years of experience to personalized, in-home music lessons tailored to each student's pace and goals. Whether you're a preschooler or an adult picking up an instrument for the first time, lessons are scheduled at your convenience and held in the comfort of your own home. Each student receives guidance on instrument setup and acquisition, and standalone audition and performance preparation is also available.

  • Member of the Richmond Philharmonic
  • Studied at University of Wyoming and Boise State University
  • Performed with over a dozen professional symphonies across the United States
